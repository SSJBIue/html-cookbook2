Moroccan Lamb Tagine

Ingredients

  • 1 kg lamb shoulder, cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp each: cumin, cinnamon, ginger, paprika
  • 200 g dried apricots
  • 50 g almonds
  • 400 ml stock
  • Fresh coriander

Method

  1. Brown lamb in oil, then remove.
  2. Sauté onion, garlic, and spices. Return lamb.
  3. Add stock, cover, simmer 1.5–2 hours.
  4. Add apricots and almonds in last 20 min.
  5. Garnish with coriander, serve with couscous.
