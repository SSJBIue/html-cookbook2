Wagyu Beef Tataki

Ingredients

  • 250 g Wagyu beef
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 small piece ginger, grated
  • Pickled daikon, shiso, sesame seeds (garnish)

Method

  1. Sear Wagyu on all sides (30â€“45 sec). Chill, then slice thinly.
  2. Mix soy, mirin, vinegar, oil, and ginger into dressing.
  3. Arrange beef slices, drizzle dressing, garnish with shiso and daikon.
