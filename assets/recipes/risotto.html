Risotto al Tartufo Bianco (White Truffle Risotto)

Ingredients

  • 300 g carnaroli rice
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 50 g butter
  • 1 L vegetable stock
  • 100 ml dry white wine
  • 80 g Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated
  • White truffle or truffle oil

Method

  1. Saut√© onion in half the butter until translucent.
  2. Add rice, toast lightly, then deglaze with wine.
  3. Gradually add hot stock, stirring until creamy but al dente.
  4. Stir in remaining butter and Parmesan.
  5. Finish with shaved truffle or drizzle of truffle oil.
