La Delizia ai Mirtilli (Blueberry Cheesecake)

Ingredients

  • 250 g digestive biscuits
  • 120 g unsalted butter, melted
  • 500 g ricotta cheese
  • 200 g mascarpone
  • 150 g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 200 g fresh blueberries
  • 100 g blueberry jam

Method

  1. Crush biscuits and combine with melted butter. Press into a springform tin.
  2. Beat ricotta, mascarpone, sugar, eggs, and lemon zest until smooth. Pour over the base.
  3. Bake at 160°C (320°F) for 50 minutes. Cool completely.
  4. Warm jam, fold in blueberries, and spoon over cheesecake before serving.
