Duck à l’Orange

Ingredients

  • 2 duck breasts, skin scored
  • Salt and pepper
  • 150 ml fresh orange juice
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 50 ml chicken stock
  • 30 g sugar
  • 30 ml white wine vinegar
  • 30 g butter

Method

  1. Season duck, sear skin-side down until crisp, then finish in oven (8–10 min at 180°C / 350°F).
  2. Caramelize sugar with vinegar, deglaze with orange juice, zest, and stock. Reduce until syrupy.
  3. Whisk in butter, slice duck, and serve with sauce.
