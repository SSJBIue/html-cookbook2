Benicio's Cookbook

This cookbook is consisted of recipes from about 50 years of kitchen experience

by the great Michelin Star, Chef Gionn Benicio. His dedication in serving people.

La Delizia ai Mirtilli

“A luscious, velvety cheesecake infused with

creamy ricotta and crowned with a vibrant blueberry compote,

balancing delicate sweetness with a touch of tart elegance."

Risotto al Tartufo Bianco

“Creamy carnaroli risotto delicately infused with white truffle essence,

finished with aged Parmigiano-Reggiano.”

Wagyu Beef Tataki

“Lightly seared Wagyu beef slices with ponzu dressing, garnished with pickled daikon and shiso.”

Duck à l’Orange

“Crisp-skinned duck breast glazed with a bittersweet orange reduction, paired with

glazed carrots and pommes purée.”

Moroccan Lamb Tagine

“Slow-cooked lamb with apricots, almonds, and aromatic spices, served in a traditional clay tagine.”
